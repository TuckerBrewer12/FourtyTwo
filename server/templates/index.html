📖 How to Play 42

Overview

42 is a trick-taking domino game for 4 players (2 teams). Your goal is to earn points through tricks and count dominoes.

Setup

  • 28 double-six dominoes are shuffled and each player draws 7 tiles.
  • Players sit opposite their partner: P1 & P3 vs P2 & P4.
  • The player to the dealer's left bids first.

Bidding

  • Minimum bid is 30. You can pass if you can't beat the current high bid.
  • Bid Low (0) to play without trump — your team must score no count dominoes.
  • Bid 42 (slam) — your team must capture all 42 points.
  • If all 4 players pass, the dealer is forced to bid 30.
  • The highest bidder declares trump and leads the first trick.

Trump

Once a trump suit is declared, all 7 dominoes of that suit rank above all others. The double of the trump suit is highest, then 6, 5, 4, 3, 2, 1, 0 (blank). A trump domino belongs only to the trump suit for that hand.

Trick Play

  • Leader plays any tile. Others must follow suit if able.
  • If the lead tile contains trump on either end, everyone must play trump.
  • If you can't follow suit, you may play any tile.
  • Highest trump wins; otherwise highest tile of the lead suit wins.
  • Winner of each trick leads the next.

Scoring

  • 7 tricks × 1 point each + 35 count domino points = 42 points per hand.
  • Bid made: Both teams score their actual points for the hand.
  • Bid failed: Bidding team scores 0; opponents score the bid amount + their own points.

Win Condition

  • 250-Point game: First team to reach 250 cumulative points wins. If both teams pass 250 on the same hand, the bidding team wins.
  • 7-Mark game: Each hand won earns 1 mark; first team to 7 marks wins.
